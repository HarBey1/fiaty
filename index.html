Displayed live mid-market rate

Every day, we display the live mid-market rate on our mobile and web app, serving as a guide as you explore available offers on our platform. Be rest assured that the displayed rate is true and does not include any markups whatsoever.

Access multiple deals with great rates

Unlike other platforms where you’re stuck with the offer you’re presented with, on FiatMatch, you’ll have access to several offers from people like you looking to exchange and send money quickly at fair rates.

Set your rate

Don’t like any of the available offers? No problem. Set your own rate and we’ll match you with community members that meet your terms. Our desire is that you feel happy after completing every transfer, knowing that you got the best deal possible.

Secured by escrow

We act as an escrow to ensure both parties fulfill the terms of their agreement. This means, you won’t send money to strangers neither will you share your personal details with them. You work hard for your money, you shouldn’t lose it to scams.

Local rather than cross-border

Each transfer is processed as a local rather than international transfer, thus saving you huge costs on cross-border fees. This means you get to do more with your money.

Choose what suits you best

Never feel stuck at any time you need to send money abroad. Choose from any of our three methods - select from available offers, post your offer or use Fiatmatch’s own offer.

What is FiatMatch

+ -

FiatMatch is a peer to peer money transfer marketplace. With FiatMatch, you can exchange currencies with people just like you at a good exchange rate and low fee, saving you time and money.

Do I need to create an account?

+ -

Yes, you do. The account creation process on FiatMatch is straightforward and fast, it should only take a few minutes.

Is there an identity verification process?

+ -

Yes, there is. We verify everyone who creates an account with us. This is to help keep every member of our community safe.

What is the FiatMatch Marketplace?

+ -

This is our secure online marketplace where you can explore currency exchange offers or create an offer that gets matched to people just like you who have been verified.
